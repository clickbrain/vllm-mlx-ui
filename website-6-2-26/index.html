Build the future of your internet — without stitching together dozens of tools.

Kilroy is a single desktop application for building, automating, launching, and sharing apps, AI workflows, and decentralized interactions. It combines visual builders, JavaScript extensibility, and end-to-end encrypted swarm messaging in one place.
